Problem

Through our research we have observed that our product was not reaching our intended users due to a lack of engagement on the site. Also, the imagery and language was focused on big mountains and gnarly skiers, which is not our target audience. This is resulting in people recreating in avalanche terrain without the proper level of education and can lead to unnecessary accidents and fatalities.

Research

We conducted in-depth interviews, surveys and researched literature in an effort to better understand the users. We were focused on the levels of avalanche awareness and training our target audience has, how they access safety information, and if they would be interested in learning opportunities.
